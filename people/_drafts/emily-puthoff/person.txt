Bio: <p><strong>Emily Puthoff</strong> is a sculpture professor and co-director of the Sculpture Program at SUNY New Paltz where she mentors students in sculpting sustainable lives and careers. As co-founder of Hudson Valley Bee Habitat (HVBH), she creatively inspires the conservation of pollinators by collaborating with communities to create public pollinator gardens and sculptures. Passionate about fostering a more just, wild, and regenerative future, Emily is committed to nurturing beauty, sustenance, and care in both the local community and the broader ecosystem through her pedagogy and creative conservation efforts at Hudson Valley Bee Habitat.</p>
