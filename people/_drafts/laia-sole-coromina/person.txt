Bio: <p><strong>Laia Solé Coromina </strong>is a visual artist, researcher and professor of Art Education in the department of Didàctica de les Arts i les Ciències at Universitat de Vic-Universitat Central de Catalunya. She has investigated contemporary art practices as sites of learning, creative barriers in higher education students, and is currently involved in research projects on art and sustainability. Her work has been exhibited at the Drawing Center in New York City, Cuchifritos Gallery in New York City, Fundació Chirivella-Soriano in València, Arts Santa Mònica in Barcelona, among others. She earned a Fine Arts degree from Universitat de Barcelona and a doctorate degree from Teachers College Columbia University.</p>
