Bio: <p><strong>Libby Roderick</strong> is Director Emerita of the Difficult Dialogues Initiative and co-founder of the <u><a href="https://www.uaa.alaska.edu/academics/office-of-academic-affairs/faculty-development-instructional-support/index.cshtml">faculty development</a></u> center at the University of Alaska Anchorage and of the Difficult Dialogues National Resource Center, which builds capacities for university communities to engage on the critical issues of our times.&nbsp; She is Associate Editor of<a href="https://www.uaa.alaska.edu/academics/institutional-effectiveness/departments/center-for-advancing-faculty-excellence/difficult-dialogues/handbooks/start-talking/"> <em>Start Talking:&nbsp; A Handbook for Engaging Difficult Dialogues in Higher Education</em></a><em>, </em>co-author of<u><a href="https://www.uaa.alaska.edu/academics/institutional-effectiveness/departments/center-for-advancing-faculty-excellence/difficult-dialogues/_documents/Stop_Talking.pdf"><em> Stop Talking: Indigenous Ways of Teaching and Learning and Difficult Dialogues in Higher Education</em></a></u><em>; </em>and<em> </em>editor of<a href="https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/alaska-native-cultures-and-issues-libby-roderick/1021110648"><em> </em></a><u><a href="https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/alaska-native-cultures-and-issues-libby-roderick/1021110648"><em>Alaska Native Cultures and Issues</em></a></u><em> </em>and<a href="https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/toxic-friday-libby-roderick/1123819056?ean=9780970284549"> <em>Toxic Friday: Resources for Addressing Faculty Bullying in Higher Education</em></a>. Libby is an internationally recognized workshop leader,<a href="http://www.libbyroderick.com/"> </a><u><a href="http://www.libbyroderick.com/">singer/songwriter</a></u>, and activist with degrees from Yale and&nbsp; Alaska Pacific University<strong>.&nbsp; </strong><a href="https://libbyroderick.com/"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></a><u><a href="https://libbyroderick.com/">https://libbyroderick.com/</a></u></p>
