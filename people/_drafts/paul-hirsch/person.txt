Bio: <p><strong>Paul Hirsch </strong>is Associate Professor and Graduate Program Director in the Department of Environmental Studies at SUNY Environmental Science and Forestry (ESF). He designs and facilitates strategies to address complexity in environmental problem-solving. &nbsp; His current work is on opportunities for trust-building across diverse people with shared connections to natural places and environmental systems.&nbsp; At ESF, Dr. Hirsch oversees the development of online graduate programs in Environmental Leadership, Justice, and Communication.&nbsp; He holds a PhD in Public Policy from Georgia Tech, an MS from the Odum School of Ecology at the University of Georgia, and a BS from Cornell University.&nbsp;</p>
