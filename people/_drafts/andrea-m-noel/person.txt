Bio: <p><strong>Andrea M. Noel</strong>, PhD, is Professor Emerita in the Department of Childhood and Early Childhood Education&nbsp; =at the State University of New York at New Paltz. She has expertise in Child Learning and Development and has much international experience including Australia, Germany, as a Fulbright Scholar in Hungary, and most recently as a grant recipient from the Polish National Agency for Academic Exchange. Dr. Noel was chosen as a Sustainability Fellow at SUNY New Paltz, and infused sustainability content into almost all her classes. She has published widely in the fields of early childhood education and learning.</p>
